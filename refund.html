1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all digital services, web development packages, UI modules, and care plans provided by Zoryvex Studio. We encourage you to read this policy together with our standard Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy agreements.

2. Services Covered

This policy governs billing terms for our custom digital offerings:

3. Our Refund Philosophy

💡 Individual Stage-Based Assessment:

Every project requires planning, research, design, coding resources, and developer allocations. Because our services are highly customized for each individual business goal, refund decisions are evaluated based on the current milestone stage of the project rather than a rigid fixed rule.

Our primary goal is always to address and resolve UI/UX issues, content adjustments, or technical concerns professionally through review adjustments before considering cancellation or a refund.

4. Eligible for Refund

A refund or partial credit may be issued under the following conditions:

  • Accidental Overpayment: Duplicate transactions or payment errors (subject to merchant clearance).
  • Unstarted Projects: Project cancellation requested before planning, setup, or wireframes begin.
  • Agency Delays: In the rare event Zoryvex Studio is unable to start or schedule the project within standard timelines.
  • Mutual Agreement: Both parties agree in writing to cancel the project prior to code setup.

* Note: Refund eligibility and percentage calculations are reviewed individually for every request.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are generally unavailable once active project tasks begin. This includes:

  • Active project planning, roadmap creation, or wireframing.
  • UI/UX layout mockups created and shared.
  • Coding, HTML, CSS structure, or database integrations started.
  • Client-approved milestones already delivered.
  • Hosting server registration, DNS setup, or domains purchased.
  • Premium software, layouts, templates, or licenses bought.
  • Delays caused by client's late responses or missing files.
  • Client requests features changes after development has started.

"Customized digital systems require significant time, resource scheduling, and code configuration. Once planning or coding work has begun, refunds are limited or unavailable depending on the completed project progress."

6. Advance Payment Policy

Zoryvex Studio initiates project phases only after the agreed advance payment (typically 40%) is cleared. This advance deposit reserves dedicated designer time, coder availability, and project planning assets.

Once project resources have been scheduled and visual drafting has commenced, this advance payment is non-refundable unless agreed otherwise in writing.

7. Project Cancellation

Clients may request cancellation of their active agreements at any time.

If a project is cancelled mid-way through development, any refunds, if eligible, will be calculated after subtracting completed milestone costs, third-party software purchases, server configuration charges, and actual hours spent. Any finished layout code or assets can be delivered to the client relative to the payments cleared.

8. Third-Party Products & Services

To make websites live and functional, we rely on third-party digital infrastructure. The following items are generally **non-refundable** because charges are processed directly by external registers:

Domain Name Registrations Web Hosting Allocations SSL Security Certificates Premium Theme/Plugin Licenses API Usage Fees Payment Gateway Fees

Any billing adjustments for these integrations are subject directly to the respective providers' policies.

9. How to Request a Refund

We keep the cancellation process simple. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Contact Support

Email our billing team at contact@zoryvex.com.

Step 2: Provide Details

Include your full name, project title, invoice number, payment details, and explanation for refund.

Step 3: Evaluation

Our team reviews the request, calculates active milestone development hours, and assesses eligibility.

Step 4: Decision

A detailed status report and refund calculation breakdown is shared with the client.

Step 5: Processing

If approved, funds are returned directly to the client's original transaction channel.

10. Refund Processing Time

11. Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with your website layouts or support services, Zoryvex Studio encourages discussing the issues with our project managers before filing payment chargebacks or taking legal action. We believe in resolving concerns fairly, professionally, and mutually to protect long-term partnerships.

12. Changes to This Policy

Zoryvex Studio reserves the right to update this Refund Policy document to align with changing tax structures or merchant portal guidelines. The latest updates are always indicated by shifting the "Last Updated" date at the header.
