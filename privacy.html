By accessing our website or using our digital solutions, you agree to the collection, storage, and practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any terms, please discontinue use of our site and services.

4. Cookies Policy

Cookies are tiny data files saved in your browser that help us recognize you when you return. Zoryvex Studio uses cookies exclusively to:

  • Deliver a faster browsing speed using cached data sheets.
  • Track anonymized statistics on page navigation (using Google Analytics).
  • Remember website theme settings (Light vs Dark mode preferences).
  • Protect website authentication session codes.

7. Data Sharing

🛑 Our Zero-Selling Promise:

Zoryvex Studio will NEVER sell, trade, or rent your personal information, email records, or phone registers to marketing brokers or third-party database sellers.

Information is only shared under these strict circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: When required by Indian authorities under legal warrants.
  • Service Providers: With hosting nodes or UPI gateway APIs to complete active project tasks.
  • Explicit Consent: Whenever you instruct us to transfer data elsewhere.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data files only as long as necessary to complete your website design projects, provide active maintenance support, comply with tax guidelines, resolve contract disputes, and guarantee ongoing secure customer backup records.

9. Your Rights

You own your information. Under data protection policies, you have complete authority to:

Access & Copy

Request a complete export sheet of all personal records we store.

Correct & Update

Change outdated names, phone numbers, or corporate emails.

Request Deletion

Instruct us to erase all details from our backup databases.

Withdraw Consent

Cancel cookie setups or opt-out of notifications at any time.

10. Children's Privacy

Our web design and digital systems are designed exclusively for business clients, startups, and adult freelancers. We do not knowingly collect or target any information from children under 13 years of age. If you suspect a minor has submitted personal files, contact us immediately to erase the records.
