How Your Final Price Is Calculated

Every proposal is tailored specifically to your project requirements. You only pay for the features your business actually needs.

Business & Website Type

Estimates depend on whether you are a local storefront, a SaaS startup, or a product fashion line.

Website Size (Pages)

A simple 5-page setup has a different structural complexity compared to a massive 20-page portal.

Custom Enhancements

Integrating features like checkout gateways, calendars, customized admin dashboards, and dynamic databases.

Hosting, Domain & SEO

Choosing proper hosting servers, custom business email setups, and detailed local SEO requirements.

Launch Timeline

Urgent schedules requiring rush setups (3-5 days) have additional speed allocations.

Maintenance & Growth

Weekly file backups, security updates, and future page scalability expansions.
